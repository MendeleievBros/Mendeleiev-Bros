What do you have to do?

In this game you have to avoid the walls which appear.

How to play?

With the key w Jacinto goes up and when you don't press any key Jacinto goes down.

What is the meaning of the things that appear in the screen?

The game has two main stages, the menu and the game. When you start to play in the screen appears a periodic table with the elements (levels) which are available. In the game there are two sticks who show: The stick which is in top shows the fuel The stick ehich is under the first shows your points, when it is full you pass the level.
